About me

Hi, I'm Nina Törő, a UX/UI designer and (web) developer.

I was fascinated by screens from a very young age (like many other GenZ), but it has become my main hobby for more than 20 years now. I've written blogs, built websites, developed apps, and now I'm a designer/developer who simply loves mixing these two fields, technology and art.

I guess you could say I bring the best of both worlds: the analytical and critical thinking based on logic from my engineering background, and the creative and open minded, brave enough to try new ways of thinking from my design background.

If you're into the formal details, check out my LinkedIn. If you’d rather see the fun side, head over to my Instagram.
