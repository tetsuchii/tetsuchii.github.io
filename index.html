About me

Hi, I'm Nina Törő, a UX/UI designer and web developer.

I specialize in creating user-friendly digital experiences with a passion for clean, minimalistic design and intuitive interactions.

I earned my degree in Computer Science Engineering from BME in 2019 and began my career as a junior front-end developer. However, I quickly realized that my true passion lays in planning and prioritizing user needs. I wanted to pursue a field that was both research-driven and visually creative—one that allowed me to connect more with people. My journey into UX design began with completing the Google UX course, followed by dedicated hard work. Now, I’m a first-year Interaction Design MA student at MOME, continuing to deepen my expertise.
