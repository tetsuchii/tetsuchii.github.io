Phoca

your caregiver self-care companion

About the Project

Phoca is a mobile application designed to support caregivers in managing their well-being while taking care of their loved ones. Caregivers often face high levels of stress, burnout, and neglect of their own needs, which can negatively impact their physical and mental health. Phoca helps caregivers by providing them with easy access to self-care tools, reminders, and resources that enable them to prioritize their own health, ensuring they can better care for others.

The Problem

The question that underpinned my semester's work was how to empower women in rural communities to become key players in social and economic resilience. I felt that in order to achieve this goal, it was important to recognise the phenomenon of what we call 'invisible work', which can affect rural women even more because of our social and cultural norms. A large and significant part of this invisible work is the problem of the sandwich generation, which means that these women often have to care for both future and past generations, raising children and taking on the role of carer for their elderly relatives. In Hungary, this is partly due to housing problems - there are few homes for the elderly, so prices are high - and partly to our cultural norms, as mentioned above, which mean that the family is responsible for helping an elderly relative, and in three quarters of cases this is the responsibility of women.

Research

In order to support my hypotheses and obtain the facts and data mentioned above, I used a variety of research approaches, both primary and secondary research techniques, to gain an insight into the topic with a breadth and depth that helped to highlight the key issues faced by rural women caring for elderly relatives.

It was an important observation that these people do not identify themselves as carers, they do not give a collective name to this set of tasks, they simply feel it is their duty for various reasons.

Through this process, I identified four key pain points that provided a deeper understanding of the situation and inspired me with ideas on how to address and alleviate these issues effectively.
